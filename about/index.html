Our mission is to offer accounting and ERP solutions for the craft beverage industry. From bank reconciliations to high level software implementations, we have an answer. Our team includes industry specific accounting professionals, Certified Quickbooks Pro Advisors, and Ekos Brewmaster Trusted Advisors. We are the exclusive business partner of GlobalBrew ERP.

Cheers ERP Solutions, Inc. is located in Portland, OR and is a proud supporter of great local craft here in the beautiful Pacific Northwest and across the country as members of the Brewers Association and C-BIG (Craft Beverage Industry Group).

Suzanne Feucht

Founder & President

Suzanne has over twenty-five years of experience as a contractaccountant/controller and software implementation professional with adiverse background in ERP systems. Over the past five years, Suzanne has been involved in the craft beverage industry working with microbrewers and distillers. Using software systems including Quickbooks, Brewmaster, and GlobalBrew, Suzanne’s focus is on establishing accounting solutions that generate a relevant financial picture for a dynamic, growing industry.

Andrew Sparks

CEO

Andrew has five years of experience in the craft beer industry as Chief Financial Officer and Co-Founder of Base Camp Brewing Company. The experience gained from working at a startup brewery has only served to deepen his passion for craft beer and the culture and creativity that surrounds it.

Amanda Sparks

VP, Operations

Amanda has been working in the craft beer industry for four years. Working for a startup brewery, she’s worn many hats from Tap Room Manager, to Office Liaison, to Project Manager. When she puts down the IPA or glass of red, you’ll find her on the Gorge trails with her husband and two dogs.

Alex Nelson

VP, Projects & Marketing

Alex is an experienced marketing and project management professional with a diverse background in several industries, including three years of experience working regionally with Anheuser-Busch InBev. A craft beer enthusiast, Alex finds inspiration from the creative minds moving the industry forward.

Ernie Bootsma

VP, Business Development

Ernie is an attorney with twenty-five years of experience working with startup and emerging businesses.
